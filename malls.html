Malls in Delhi

Select Citywalk, Saket

Location: Saket

Nearest Metro Station: Saket Metro Station

Select Citywalk

The biggest mall in Delhi, Select City Walk is one of its kind. The mall is as posh as it can get! International luxury brands such as H&M, Dior, Chanel, Sephora, Zara, La Senza, Aldo, Forever 21 are available here. Food outlets of all kinds, ranging from Indian to Continental, snacks to desserts, are here to satisfy your taste buds when you take a break from shopping. The outside of the mall is a vast open area with fountains and gardens, where flea markets, open-air discussions and other events bring life to the city.

Address: A-3, Distt. Centre, Saket, New Delhi

  • Mall Timings: 10:00 AM to 11:00 PM

    • DLF Promenade, Vasant Kunj

    Location: Vasant Kunj

    Nearest Metro Station: Vasant Vihar Metro Station

    DLF Promenade

    An upscale mall in Delhi, DLF Promenade offers branded luxury products of Zara, Sephora, Marks & Spencers, and expensive ones like Chanel Beauty, BCBGeneration, Juicy Couture and Hunkemoller. The delicious options here include some exquisite outlets like Nandos, TGIF, The Big Chill and Smoke House Deli, etc. This mall in Delhi houses PVR Icon Cinemas, and a kids zone named Kiddyland. The outside infrastructure includes fountains and gardens, and an open-air hangout spot called ‘The Hub’.

    Address: 3, Nelson Mandela Marg, Vasant Kunj, New Delhi

  • Mall Timings: 10:00 AM to 11:00 PM

    • Pacific Mall, Subhash Nagar

    Location: Subhash Nagar

    Nearest Metro Station: Subhash Nagar Metro Station

    Pacific Mall

    Pacific Mall is in West Delhi houses top-notch national and international labels including Tommy Hilfiger, Promod, ONLY, Mango, Forever 21, MAC, United Colors of Benetton, Calvin Klein, Vero Moda, Jack & Jones, and French Connection. Themed cafes such as Hinglish-Cafe Beach Car and Castle’s Barbecue are also found here. Recently, flea markets and other open-air events have started taking place here.

    Address: Tagore Garden, Najafgarh Road, Subhash Nagar, New Delhi

  • Mall Timings: 10:00 AM to 11:00 PM

    • Ansal Plaza Mall, Andrews Ganj

    Location: Andrews Ganj

    Nearest Metro Station: South Extension Metro Station

    Ansal Plaza

    Ansal Plaza is one of the oldest malls in Delhi. It is a unique one, because it does not feature premium brands, but has stores of its own kind. Ansal Plaza has some fast food joints, which make it a popular hangout destination for college students. It features a Decathlon store. The main attraction of Ansal Plaza is the huge amphitheatre, located in the middle of the mall. This amphitheatre gives budding artists a practice area, and even serves as a group study joint.

    Address: Andrews Ganj, Khel Gaon Marg, South Delhi

  • Mall Timings: Monday to Friday - 11 AM to 11 PM. Saturday and Sunday - 11 AM to 12 AM.

    FAQs

    What are the best malls in Delhi?

    Some popular malls in Delhi include:

    • Select Citywalk, Saket
    • DLF Promenade, Vasant Kunj
    • Pacific Mall, Subhash Nagar
    • Ansal Plaza Mall, Andrews Ganj
    What are the timings for malls in Delhi?

    Typical mall timings in Delhi are:

    • Monday to Thursday: 10am - 10pm
    • Friday to Sunday: 10am - 11pm
    Are Delhi malls open on public holidays?

    Yes, most malls in Delhi remain open on public holidays, but may have restricted timings.

    Can I park my vehicle at Delhi malls?

    Yes, most malls in Delhi offer paid parking facilities.

    Are Delhi malls wheelchair accessible?

    Yes, most modern malls in Delhi are wheelchair accessible with ramps and elevators.
